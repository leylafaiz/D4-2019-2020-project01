SuperBee's side: step-3-
The exchange starts!

Superbee will be taking two important things from the flower:
-First of all, her nectar because it is so delicious, and it will help the bee produce the honey it needs later.
-Second of all, SuperBee will find itself covered with pollen, to help the flowers reproduce and also to feed larvae with beebread: a mixture of pollen and nectar.
